The Profile

Founder & CEO – Learning Without Walls

Dr. Krishnan Chandrasekharan is a globally recognized L&D transformation leader, executive coach, and leadership strategist with over **25 years of experience** in human behavior and organizational development.

As the Founder & CEO of Learning Without Walls, Dr. Krishnan leads an initiative focused on reimagining learning beyond traditional classrooms. His philosophy emphasizes that learning should not be confined to training programs but should evolve into continuous capability-building systems that drive measurable performance.

Throughout his career, he has trained and coached more than 150,000 professionals and students globally, ranging from fresh graduates to senior leaders and CXOs across industries. His work spans India, the UK, the US, and the EMEA region.

His programs are known for combining structured frameworks with highly interactive learning methods such as real-world simulations and reflective coaching practices. He also explores how Artificial Intelligence can enhance learning Diagnostics and personalize development journeys.

Beyond corporate consulting, Dr. Krishnan actively contributes to the global learning ecosystem through thought leadership and trainer development initiatives, empowering educators to create meaningful impact.
